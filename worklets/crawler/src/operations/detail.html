Notebook.ai Product Information

What is Notebook.ai?

Notebook.ai is a set of tools for writers and roleplayers to create magnificent universes — and everything within them. It's a smart notebook for worldbuilders that grows with your ideas and collaborates with you. With a free account, users have access to core building blocks of fictional universes, personalized writing prompts, unlimited document storage, and a community of other worldbuilders.

How to use Notebook.ai?

Sign up for a free account on Notebook.ai. Start creating universes, characters, locations, and items. Use the integrated novel-writing software to cross-reference notebook pages and documents. Invite others to review and collaborate on your pages.
